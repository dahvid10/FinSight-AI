# FinSight AI

FinSight AI is an intelligent personal finance application that uses the Gemini API to provide personalized financial insights and budget analysis based on your income, expenses, and savings goals.

## Features

- **Personalized Budget Analysis:** Enter your income, expenses, and savings goals to receive a comprehensive budget plan.
- **Multi-City Comparison:** Compare your financial outlook in up to 5 different cities side-by-side to understand cost of living differences.
- **AI-Powered Insights:** The app estimates your after-tax income based on your location and provides a detailed breakdown of your finances.
- **Actionable Advice:** Get tailored recommendations for saving money and improving your cash flow.
- **Interactive Financial Chat:** Chat with an AI assistant to ask questions and explore your financial options in more detail.
